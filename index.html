Services

Hair Dye

  • Full Color - Dye all of your hair a certain color or choose to wild and spruce up your looks with a beautiful ombre!

    Cost starting from $70 - $140
    Duration: ~1 hour

  • Highlights - Change how bright your hair looks by dyeing lighter or darker strands of hair or adding extra colors to accent your hair like in the picture.

    Cost starting from $60 - $150
    Duration: 1 hour 15 min - 2 hours

  • Tips/Partial Highlights - Dye the tips or small sections of your hair only. Best choice if you aren't sure if you want a full dye.

    Costs starts from $40 - $80
    Duration: 45 min - 1 hour

Prices and Duration increase depending on the amount of colors, complexity, hair length and coverage. Extra colors add $20-$40.

Hair Cut And Styling

  • Short - Cut up until hair length is around chin-length or shorter. Typically includes undercuts, short bobs, mullets and pixie cuts.

    Cost starting from $35 - $140
    Duration: 15 - 45 min

  • Medium/Long - Sholder/Neck-length or longer cuts which allow for increasingly complex styling and updos.

    Cost starting from $60 - $150
    Duration: 30 min - 1 hour 15 min

  • Trim - Freshen up your current haircut and remove damaged ends.

    Set price of $15 or $10 for kids
    Duration: 15 min

Kid's start from $25, Men's from $35 and Women's from $45 and increase depending on hair type and style complexity.

Facial/Make-up

  • Full Facial - Cleanse and refresh your face with the full set of deep cleansing, exfoliating, mask and oil massage. You'll leave the salon stress free!

    Cost starting from $100
    Duration: 1 hour - 1 hour 45 min

  • Personalized Makeup - Our professional artists will personalize your makeup application to best suit your features and skin tone, enhancing your natural beauty.

    Costs starting from $60 - $90
    Duration: 30 min - 1 hour

  • Personalized Makeup + Lesson - Get a Personalized makeup session while learning everything you need to know about applying makeup for yourself from our experts.

    Costs $80 - $100
    Duration: ~1 hour

We also do bridal makeup applications.

Mani-/Pedi- cure

  • Manicure - Filing and buffing, shaping, cuticle care and ending with a polish.

    Standard: $15 - $35; Deluxe: $30 - $40
    Duration: 30 min - 1 hour

  • Pedicure - Trimming and filing, complete cuticle care, foot bath, exfoliation and massage, ending with a polish.

    Standard: $15 - $35; Deluxe: $35 - $45
    Duration: 30 min - 1 hour 30 min

  • Polish - Freshen up your current polish by applying another coat or change to a new color.

    Costs $5 - $20 depending on design and polish type (Acrylic, French or Gel)
    Duration: 5 - 15 min
